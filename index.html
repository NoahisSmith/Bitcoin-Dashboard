Mayer Multiple
Price ÷ 200-day moving average. A simple but reliable gauge of how stretched price is from its own trend. Below 0.6 has historically marked deep-bear accumulation zones; above 2.4 has coincided with cycle-top euphoria. Used here in place of MVRV Z-Score, which needs a "realized cap" series that no longer has a free, keyless, full-history source (see README) — Mayer Multiple targets a similar over/undervaluation signal using only price data.

Puell Multiple
Daily miner revenue in USD ÷ 365-day MA of daily miner revenue. Miners are the natural sellers of BTC. High Puell values (>4) mean miners are earning unusually high revenue — historically a sell signal. Low values (<0.5) indicate miner capitulation and accumulation opportunities.
